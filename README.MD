# Electric Vehicle Population Analysis 🚗⚡

Exploratory data analysis on electric vehicle registrations in the United States.

## Key Findings
- Total EVs analyzed: 150,000+
- Most popular brand: Tesla
- Significant growth in EV adoption after 2020

## Visualizations
- Top 10 most popular EV brands
- EV registrations growth by year
- Electric vehicle types distribution
- Top 10 most popular EV models
